Chinese food


There are four kinds of Chinese food, Cantonese cuisine, Beijing cuisine, Shanghai cuisine and Sichuan cuisine.

Cantonese cuisine: Light taste

Beijing cuisine: inheriting the tradition of court cuisine

Shanghai cuisine: thick and sweet taste

Sichuan cuisine: pungent

mannar of eating food


1 Leave food
2 start eating from one's superiors
3 eat a platter separately
4 use a new plate
5 do not eat soup or noodles with a noise
Let's follow the rule and having fun eating meal with Chinese.
